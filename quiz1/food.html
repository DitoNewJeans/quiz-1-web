    Foods

    Kerak Telor

    Kerak telor (English: Egg crust) is a Betawi traditional spicy omelette dish in Indonesian cuisine. It is made from glutinous rice cooked with egg and served with serundeng (fried shredded coconut), fried shallots and dried shrimp as topping.

    Nasi Uduk

    Nasi uduk (Indonesian: nasi uduk) is an Indonesian style steamed rice cooked in coconut milk dish, especially popular in Betawi cuisine.
